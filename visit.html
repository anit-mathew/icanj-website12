What To Expect

Warm Welcome

Our greeters team is ready to meet you with a smile, answer your questions, and help you find your seat comfortably.

Come As You Are

No dress code here. Some come in jeans, others in Sunday best — what matters is your heart, not your outfit.

Dynamic Worship

Expect passionate, Spirit-filled worship that creates an atmosphere of genuine encounter with God.

About 90 Minutes

Our services run approximately 90 minutes — enough time to worship, hear the Word, and connect with others.

Easy Parking

Free parking is available right at the church building. We're easy to find at 315 State Street, Hackensack.

For Families

Kids Are Welcome Here

We believe children deserve more than childcare during church. Our Kids Ministry runs every Sunday morning — a safe, fun, and faith-building environment designed just for them.
