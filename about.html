Are

Passionate for God. Compassionate for humanity. Committed to perfecting the saints and transforming lives through faithful Biblical teaching, soul-saving witness, and Spirit-filled worship. Our Mission — ICANJ
Our Vision

A Christ-centered body of believers of every generation, with a passion for God and compassion for humanity, existing for the perfecting of saints and the transformation of all — through faithful Biblical teaching, soul-saving witnessing, and Spirit-filled worship.

As we approach 2033 — marking 2,000 years of the birth of the Church — we are striving to bring every generation to maturity in Christ, reaching further into every nation, from New Jersey and beyond.

Our History

Rooted in Faith Since 1987

India Christian Assembly of New Jersey began in 1987 in Bergenfield, New Jersey — born out of a small gathering of believers with a passion for God and a burden to reach the unreached with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

From those humble beginnings, the Lord has been faithful. What started as a handful of families praying together has grown into a vibrant, Spirit-filled congregation. Today the church is rooted in Hackensack, New Jersey, at 315 State Street — a full-gospel church grounded in the teachings of the Lord Jesus Christ and His apostles.

Across the years, ICANJ has remained committed to faithful biblical teaching, soul-winning witness, and Spirit-filled worship — caring for its members, serving its community, and supporting the work of missions both locally and abroad. We give all glory to God for what He has done, and we look forward in faith to all He will continue to do through this church for generations to come.

Theology

What We Believe

Our faith rooted in Scripture and the historic Christian tradition.

01

The Holy Scriptures

The Bible is God's inspired, infallible Word and our final authority for faith and life. — Sola Scriptura

02

The Triune God

One God, eternally existing in three Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — co-equal and co-eternal.

03

Creation & the Fall

God made humanity in His image; through Adam's sin all are fallen and in need of His grace.

04

Jesus Christ

Fully God and fully man — crucified for our sins, risen bodily, now reigning and interceding at the right hand of the Father. — Solus Christus

05

Salvation by Grace

Salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone — producing a transformed life. — Sola Gratia, Sola Fide

06

The Holy Spirit

The third Person of the Trinity, who convicts, regenerates, indwells, and sanctifies believers.

07

Baptism of the Holy Spirit

A distinct empowering for witness and service, given subsequent to salvation.

08

The Initial Evidence

Speaking in other tongues is the initial physical evidence of Spirit baptism.

09

Gifts of the Spirit

The Spirit distributes gifts to build up the Church and advance the Gospel in the world.

10

Signs Following Believers

The Lord confirms His Word with signs as the Gospel is proclaimed.

11

Authority & Victory in Christ

Through the cross and resurrection, believers share in Christ's victory over sin and darkness.

12

Divine Healing

Healing is provided in Christ's redemptive work and is available to His people today.

13

The Church & Mission

The Church worships God, proclaims the Gospel, and makes disciples of all nations.

14

The Ordinances

Water baptism by immersion and the Lord's Supper, as established by Christ.

15

Marriage

The exclusive, lifelong covenant union of one man and one woman, as God designed.

16

The Return of Christ

Christ will return visibly and bodily; the dead will be raised and all will stand before God.

17

The Glory of God

All things exist for the glory of God, to whom be honor, majesty, and dominion forever. — Soli Deo Gloria

Spiritual Formation

The Growth Pathway

We are called not only to receive Christ, but to grow in Him — together.

01

Know Christ

Receive salvation by grace through faith in Jesus, repenting of sin and trusting His finished work.

02

Be Baptized

Follow the Lord in water baptism by immersion as a public confession of your faith.

03

Be Filled with the Spirit

Earnestly seek the baptism of the Holy Spirit for power to witness and to serve.

04

Grow in the Word

Take part in Bible study, Sunday School, and discipleship classes to be rooted in truth.

05

Connect in Community

Join a small group or ministry where you are known, prayed for, and encouraged.

06

Serve & Multiply

Discover your spiritual gifts, serve the body, and help lead others to Christ.

Membership

Member Expectations

Membership is a joyful commitment to belong to the body of Christ at ICANJ.

Worship Faithfully

Attend weekly worship services and gather regularly with the church family.

Pursue Holiness

Walk in obedience to God's Word, growing in Christlike character and conduct.

Give Generously

Honor God with your tithes and offerings to support His work and mission.

Serve Willingly

Use your gifts to serve in a ministry and build up the body of Christ.

Fellowship in Love

Pursue unity, encourage one another, and care for fellow members.

Honor Leadership

Pray for and submit to the spiritual leadership God has placed over the church.

Witness Boldly

Share the Gospel and support the church's mission to reach the lost.

Uphold Our Faith

Affirm and live by the church's Statement of Faith and biblical standards, including God's design for marriage and family.
